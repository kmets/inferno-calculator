Inferno is a hypothetical friendly fork protocol of AladdinDAO's CLever. The protocol offers a twist on CLever by incentivizing the debt token (fireUSD) out of the Furnace and allowing another token (BcvxCRV) to take its place. This calculator lets users determine what conditions (TVL, burn token, peg discounts, incentives, etc.) would affect the ability to lure fireUSD out of the furnace and replace it with BcvxCRV.

Have fun burning stuff and reach out if you'd like to help build Inferno!
